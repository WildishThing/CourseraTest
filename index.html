SPECIAL ANNOUNCEMENTS
Today is a Day of Prayer for the Unemployed.
Wednesday 17th Feb is Ash Wednesday, and the beginning of the Season of Lent. It is a Day of Penance for the whole Church of the Roman Rite, to be marked by fasting and abstinence from the eating of meat. There will be two Masses: 10.00am at St Joseph’s and 6.00pm at Sacred Heart. Both Masses to include the Blessing and Distribution of Ashes (to be administered by non-contact i.e. by sprinkling on the crown of the head).
